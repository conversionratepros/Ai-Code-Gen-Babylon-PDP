Hand Cream

Smeulende Fynbos Hand Cream

Herbal, woody, fruity 100 ml
R 120
1
Description

A luxuriously silky, non-greasy hand cream that will leave your hands soft, smooth and slightly scented. Created in our Soap Factory with a fast-absorbing formula, this nourishing hand cream can be applied as often as needed. It is an on-the-go essential that also makes for a wonderful small gift.

Scent Profile

Fynbos – the evergreen shrubland that clothes the mountains and lowlands of the Cape – is excellently adapted to fire. It burns rapidly and new growth rises from the ashes, shaped by flames. With perfectly balanced notes of sage, tobacco and fynbos, Smeulende Fynbos evokes the scent of late summer in the Cape mountains: a drift of resinous woodsmoke, the smouldering crackle of flames.

FRAGRANCE NOTES: fynbos, tobacco, sage
